Leon is a Legendary Brawler who has a very fast movement speed, moderate health, a long range, immense utility with his Super, and a high damage output at close range, in exchange for a moderately slow reload speed. He was the 22nd Brawler added to the game, and released alongside the global launch of Brawl Stars. He attacks by flicking out four Spinner Blades in a narrow cone that sweep and deal more damage close and less damage further away. Leon's Super makes him temporarily invisible to enemies. His first Gadget, Clone Projector, spawns a clone of himself to confuse and deal very low damage to enemies, although the conjured clone takes twice the amount of damage. His second Gadget, Lollipop Drop, places his lollipop that turns his teammates and him invisible within its area-of-effect until it is destroyed. His Mythic Gear, Lingering Smoke, increases his Super duration. His first Star Power, Smoke Trails, increases his movement speed while his Super is active. His second Star Power, Invisiheal, heals him over time while his Super is active. His Hypercharge, Limbo, allows him to stay invisible when attacking while his Super is active.


Colt is a Rare Brawler who has moderately low health, a long range, a very fast reload speed, and an inconsistently high burst damage. He was one of the 15 Brawlers who released at the first Beta launch. His attack is a long-ranged volley of six bullets. His Super is a longer-ranged volley of twelve piercing bullets that can destroy obstacles. His first Gadget, Speedloader, causes him to fire two rapid shots that slow down enemies. His second Gadget, Silver Bullet, makes his next attack a single piercing bullet that destroys obstacles and deals the damage of two of his main attack's bullets. His first Star Power, Slick Boots, passively increases his movement speed. His second Star Power, Magnum Special, increases his main attack's range and bullet speed. His Hypercharge, Dual Wielding, increases the width of his Super.


Barley is a Rare Brawler who has low health and slow reload speed, but a high burst damage potential and a long range. He was one of the 15 Brawlers who released at the first Beta launch. He attacks by lobbing a bottle of harmful liquid that splashes onto the ground and creates a small puddle on the ground that lasts for a period of time, dealing damage to enemies splashed and inside the puddle over time. His Super lobs five similar bottles on fire. These bottles travel further, cover a greater area, persist for a longer period of time, and they do not set a burning effect. His first Gadget, Sticky Syrup Mixer, creates a sticky puddle around himself that slows enemies inside it down. His second Gadget, Herbal Tonic, throws bottles around himself and teammates that heal them if they stand on the puddles. His first Star Power, Medical Use, heals him slightly when he lobs his bottle with his attack. His second Star Power, Extra Noxious, increases his main attack's damage per second. His Hypercharge, Bottled-Up Rage, makes his Super lob three big bottles of liquid that covers a larger area than his normal Super, destroying bushes in the process. Barley and his Wizard skin
can be unlocked for free by linking an account to Supercell ID.

Sirius is an Ultra Legendary Brawler who has moderately high health, a variable range and damage output, a very fast movement and reload speed, and immense utility with his Super. He was the 100th Brawler added to the game, and during his initial release he could be unlocked for free early by winning one match each with the previous 99 Brawlers. His Trait allows his Super to charge itself over time. His attack fires two projectiles at the same time, which consist of a long ranged Shadow Strike and a Starr Bomb that explodes onto the ground in a small radius. The attacks charge his Brawler Shadows. For his Super, Sirius spawns all collected Brawler Shadows, which have less health and less damage than their Brawler counterparts. He can use it to direct the Shadows into attacking other targets or send them back to him. His first Gadget, A Starr Is Born, spawns a Shadow upon impact, temporarily slowing them down in the process. His second Gadget, Master of Shadows, summons the Shadows back to Sirius, healing them in the process. His first Star Power, Dusk Runners, increases the movement speed of the Shadows. His second Star Power, The Darkest Starr, increases the amount of hits with the Shadow Strike. His Hypercharge, Unlimited Power!, increases the Shadows' health and damage.


Kaze is an Ultra Legendary Brawler with moderately high health and two modes. She was the 92nd Brawler added to the game. In her geisha aspect, she has a very fast movement speed, a variable damage output, and a very fast reload speed, and immense utility with her attack. She attacks by dashing forward and slashing, dealing moderate damage. For her Super, she throws a hand fan which summons a storm that makes her and allies invisible to enemies in it, dealing damage overtime to enemies that touch the outer edge. In her ninja aspect, she has a normal movement speed, a higher damage output at close range, and immense utility with her Super, at the expense of having a moderately low reload speed. Kaze attacks by throwing two knives that deal more damage up close. For her Super, she travels a long distance quickly. Enemies caught in her path will be temporarily marked, which detonates shortly after for high damage. For her first Gadget, Gracious Host, Kaze gains a temporary movement speed boost when switching to ninja, and recovers health when switching to geisha. For her second Gadget, Hensojutsu, Kaze becomes temporary invisible to enemies when switching to ninja, and dashes forward when switching to geisha. Her first Star Power, Advanced Techniques, temporary slows enemies down if she hits enemies in the Strike Spot in her geisha aspect, and nearby enemies will be damaged when marked targets explode in her ninja aspect. Her second Star Power, Gratuity Included, removes ammo from any enemy Brawler caught in the storm in her geisha aspect, and increases the duration of her Gadgets in her ninja aspect. Her Hypercharge, Ancient Energy, allows her Super to slightly damage enemies inside it over time in her geisha aspect, and increases the Super dash speed, while instantly damaging marked enemies in her ninja aspect.
